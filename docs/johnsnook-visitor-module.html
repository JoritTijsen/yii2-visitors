portcullis() protected method

Called on the BeforeAction event, it logs the current visitor, and if it's a new visitor, gather their ipInfo, proxy information and browser info.

Then, check their blacklist status or compares their info against the blacklist criteria.

If the visitor IS blacklisted, redirect them to the blowoff action. If they're going to the blowoff action, let them and don't redirect them so much the browser throws a fucking error.
